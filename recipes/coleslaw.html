Coleslaw


Description

Amazing Coleslaw? No-Brainer!

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) package coleslaw mix
  • ¼ cup finely chopped yellow onion
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Stir together coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, milk, buttermilk, vinegar,
    salt, and pepper in a second bowl until smooth. Pour dressing over slaw;
    stir well and chill at least 2 hours or up to overnight.
